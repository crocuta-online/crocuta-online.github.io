We are making Astropelago ,
an awesome game for you and your dawgs!
Astropelago is a chatroom game with lifesim progression elements featuring a modular fishing minigame to allow self-paced difficulty!
You'll find many strange fish when you cast your line into space...
Together with your friends, you will revitalize a broken world through the power of love and community.
Customize your character with features from multiple species and styles. You can even paint custom markings. We're cooking!
